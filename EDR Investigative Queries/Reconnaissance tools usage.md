# Reconnaissance tools usage

## Query Syntax 
### Easy 
```
Process Name:whoami.exe OR Process Name:netstat.exe OR Process Name:ping.exe OR Process Name:ipconfig.exe OR Process Name:regedit.exe OR Process Name:net.exe Process Name:schtasks.exe OR Process Name:quser.exe or Process Name:hostname.exe OR Process Name:forfiles.exe or Process Name:systeminfo.exe
```
### Advanced
```
process.file.name:whoami.exe OR process.file.name:netstat.exe OR process.file.name:ping.exe OR process.file.name:ipconfig.exe OR process.file.name:regedit.exe OR process.file.name:"net.exe Process Name:schtasks.exe" OR process.file.name:"quser.exe or Process Name:hostname.exe" OR process.file.name:"forfiles.exe or Process Name:systeminfo.exe"
```
